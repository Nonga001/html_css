About Camara Education

Camara Education is a pioneering, award-winning international charity dedicated to transforming education in under-resourced communities by leveraging the power of technology. Founded in 2005, Camara operates with a strong belief that quality education empowered by digital access can lift entire generations out of poverty.

Our Mission

To transform education using technology to empower learners and educators, promote digital inclusion, and ensure sustainable educational outcomes for marginalized communities across Africa and beyond.

Our Vision

A world where every learner has access to quality, technology-enabled education, regardless of their geographic or socio-economic background.

What We Do

  • Supply refurbished computers and educational software to schools.
  • Train teachers in digital literacy and modern pedagogical approaches.
  • Partner with ministries of education to scale proven innovations.
  • Engage in strategic advocacy for tech-driven education policies.
  • Promote sustainability through responsible recycling and reuse of ICT hardware.

Our Impact

Camara has impacted over 3.5 million students and teachers across seven African countries, providing over 150,000 computers and training more than 60,000 educators. Through strong partnerships and a commitment to sustainability, Camara continues to deliver meaningful change.

Core Values

  • Innovation: We seek creative, scalable solutions to complex challenges in education.
  • Integrity: We operate transparently and deliver on our promises.
  • Collaboration: We work with communities, governments, and partners for collective success.
  • Empowerment: We build local capacity to ensure long-term impact and ownership.
  • Inclusivity: We serve all learners, especially the most disadvantaged.

Our Global Reach

Camara has worked in countries including Ethiopia, Kenya, Lesotho, Tanzania, Zambia, and Uganda, with operations supported by hubs in Ireland, the UK, and the US.
